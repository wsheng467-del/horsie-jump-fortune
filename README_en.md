Overview
- Horsie Jump Fortune is an easy-to-pick-up Chinese New Year themed jumping game. Players tap on their phone to control a horsie on a big screen to jump across coins, "Fu" characters, spring banners and other obstacles. Longer jumps score higher; completing stages can trigger celebratory fireworks.

Core Gameplay
- Controls: Tap to jump (optional: hold to charge for a longer jump).
- Objectives: Clear obstacles and platforms such as coins, "Fu" characters, and spring banners.
- Scoring: Longer jumps earn more points. Chains and landing on special zones grant combo/bonus points.
- Stage Elements: Variable obstacle layouts, moving platforms, wind/time effects, etc.
- Rewards: Reaching certain distances or clearing stages triggers fireworks and leaderboard displays.

Features
- Chinese New Year themed art ("Fu" characters, spring banners, coins, fireworks).
- One-thumb controls ideal for quick social play.
- Expandable items and skins (e.g., new year costumes, speed boosts).
- Leaderboards and achievements for friendly competition.

Quick Tips
1. Tap/short press: small jump
2. Hold/charge: longer jump
3. In-air adjustments (optional)
4. Stable landings yield combo bonuses
